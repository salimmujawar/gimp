Possible folder structure based on your operating system...

Custom Keyboard shortcuts are stored in one of Gimp's hidden directory under Linux: 

/home/[username]/.gimp-2.8/menurc



Under Windows, path varies according to version:

C:\Documents and Settings\[Username]\.gimp-2.8\menurc under Windows XP.

C:\Program Files\GIMP 2\etc\gimp\2.0\menurc under Windows 7.

C:\Programmes\GIMP 2\etc\gimp\2.0\menurc under Windows 10.



For MAC:

users\user id\Library\Application Support\GIMP\2.10
